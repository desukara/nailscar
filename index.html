If this is an emergency

If you are in immediate danger, think you may act on suicidal thoughts, or someone is at immediate risk, contact emergency services or go to the nearest hospital immediately.

Do not wait for an email reply or a website response in a life-threatening situation.

What Nailscar Ministry is

A faith-based ministry devoted to suicide prevention awareness, survivor support, compassionate presence, and helping people find the right crisis and community resources.

Prevention and awareness

We want to speak openly and compassionately about suicide, pain, despair, warning signs, and the importance of reaching for help.

Support for survivors

We recognize the deep grief and ongoing reality faced by suicide loss survivors and others living with the aftermath.

Direction to real help

We help point people toward crisis lines, emergency services, hospitals, community supports, and other appropriate resources.

What we are not

We believe honesty builds trust. Nailscar Ministry is not a licensed mental health clinic, not psychiatric care, and not a substitute for emergency or professional treatment.

Please note: We do not diagnose, provide medical treatment, replace therapy, or function as a crisis hotline.

What we can do

We can offer faith-based encouragement, survivor understanding, pastoral perspective, compassionate listening, and direction toward the right next resource when someone is hurting.

Our role: support, awareness, spiritual care, and resource direction.

Why this ministry exists

Nailscar Ministry is shaped by firsthand experience with suicide loss and survival. This is not abstract. It comes from real pain, real grief, and the conviction that people in crisis and families in shock need compassion, honesty, and clear direction.

The heart behind this ministry is simple: to help people know they are not alone, to speak about suicide with truth and mercy, and to help point people toward life-giving help.
