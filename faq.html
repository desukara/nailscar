Frequently Asked Questions

Is it wrong to question what I was taught?

No. Honest questions can be part of healing and clarity.

Why do I still feel guilty all the time?

Many people coming out of legalism carry trained fear, shame, and hyper-responsibility. Those patterns can take time to unwind.

Does healing mean abandoning faith?

Not necessarily. For many people, healing means separating harmful control from what is actually true, good, and life-giving.
