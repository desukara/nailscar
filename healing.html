Healing

Healing from legalism can be slow and layered. It may involve grief, emotional recovery, relearning trust, and separating truth from fear-based conditioning.

Possible First Steps

  • Rest without shame
  • Name what hurt you honestly
  • Learn the difference between control and love
  • Seek safe people and healthy support
  • Give yourself permission to ask questions
