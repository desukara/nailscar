What Is Legalism?

Legalism can show up as fear-based religion, constant performance pressure, rigid control, shame, and the idea that a person’s worth depends on how well they follow rules or appear spiritually strong.

Common Signs

  • Fear of making mistakes
  • Chronic guilt and self-condemnation
  • Pressure to perform spiritually
  • Control through shame or fear
  • Confusing obedience with emotional suppression
