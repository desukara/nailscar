About Nailscar

Nailscar was created as a place for people recovering from Christian legalism, spiritual pressure, fear-based teaching, and religious control.

Why this site exists

Many people leave legalistic environments carrying deep confusion, shame, fear, and self-doubt. Some are still inside those systems and feel trapped. Others are already out, but still struggle with guilt, anxiety, or the feeling that they are never doing enough.

This site exists to offer clarity, language, and encouragement for people trying to understand what happened to them and what healing can look like.

Who this is for

Nailscar is for survivors of Christian legalism, people questioning harmful religious systems, and anyone trying to separate fear, performance, and control from what is actually true and life-giving.

What you can expect here

This site is meant to be calm, honest, and supportive. You can expect resources, explanations, healing-focused content, and space to reflect without pressure.
